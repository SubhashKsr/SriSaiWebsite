About Us

Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our customers by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust
through exceptional performance by every member of the construction team.

Sri Sai Constructions is a ISO 9001:2015 certified company

Established in 1999

M/s. Sri Sai Constructions started its operation in the year 1999 – 2000 run by a group of well experienced personnel involved in the business of Mining and Civil Engineering works. It has spanned its operations in various states of India, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Tamilnadu and Orissa. Sri Sai Constructions (S.S.C.) mainly specialized in all kind of rock and quarry works which include Drilling, Blasting, Transportation Earth work excavation, Irrigation canals, Drilling & Blasting works, Loading & Crushing of boulders, and other civil works.

We built a strong reputation for delivering a quality service, on time, and at a competitive price. We pride ourselves on being skilled and experienced in all aspects of civil construction and this, combined with our extensive knowledge of the mining market, has established us as major players in this industry.

The Company is achieved in Excavation of Rock & Soil Quantity of 1 Cr Cub/mts in the past 4 years. Presently, Company is able to achieve a quantity of 10000 – 12000 cub/mts per day with its state of the art mining equipment and imported machineries, including Compressors, Crawlers, High Capacity Excavators, Dumpers, Loaders etc., S.S.C. has Specialized Mining staffs and a team of dedicated work force.

We undertake a variety of projects for a wide range of cliental – from small private developments to large Government projects. Our unique and flexible project management systems ensure that a positive outcome is achieved regardless of size or nature of the project.
